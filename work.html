Latest Collaborations

La Descarga

In 2014 I coproduced and recorded keyboards and pianos for the Carlos Paul’s single called “La Descarga”. The recording sessions took place at “El Tano Studios” in Peterborough and it took us about two months to follow the whole process of arranging, recording templates, transcribing the music for the session players, recording each part, mixing and mastering. The track is now available in Itunes, Spotify, Soundcloud, etc. The track mixes latin styles, rock ‘n’ roll and folk, creating a really nice track.

Made In Niniru

Made in Nibiru is a project that took place in 2014. I coproduced five of their songs and played synths during the recording sessions. The style can be classified as soft or pop rock as it’s a project trying to follow the styles of bands such as: Coldplay, The Verve, Adam Lambert, etc. I helped making the arrangements, harmonizing different parts and mixing the tracks, as well as playing the keys for them in concerts. Music produced by Made in Nibiru can be found and downloaded from Soundcloud.

Todo es Posible

This year Carlos Paul released his new album called “Todo es Posible” in which I have participated in various stages of the production. I contributed with the arrangements, recorded piano, organ, synths and coproduced the material. The album mixes different styles of music involving musicians from all over the world with different playing styles creating a fantastic album. All tracks have been mastered at the legendary Abby Road studios and are available for download from Itunes, Spotify.
