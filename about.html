About Me

I was born in Valparaiso of Chile, did my primary school at “Escuela Estado de Israel” and secondary school at “Instituto Comercial Valparaiso”, leaving the institution with a qualification in book keeping.

From the age of twelve I took private guitar and piano lessons in classical and pop music.

In the year 2000 I moved to the UK to polish my English language and do some computer training at Edgware Academy of Languages and Computer Science. After being offered the opportunity to stay, I have been working as a session player supporting different bands and acts as well as having music training at City Lit, Morley College, WAES and a sound engineering course at the London Centre of Contemporary Music.

Last year I decided to take my career even further and joined City of Westminster college on an Access to Higher education course which helped me to get the credits to join this year Goldsmiths University of London on a Creative Computing degree.
